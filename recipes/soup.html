ONE-POT ARTISAN CONCHIGLIE SOUP IN CALABRESE-STYLE MEAT SAUCE

Recipe Credit: Courtesy of foodandjourneys.com

Description:

This dish is a perfect summer light dinner, a perfect lunch for college kids, or any busy bee on the go! Simply add pasta or gluten-free pasta, water and the sauce and hit a button to go!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 lbs Italian sausage, fennel (or other types)
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped finely
  • 1 medium onion, chopped finely
  • 1 medium carrot, chopped into small bits
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped finely
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper, to season
  • pinch peperoncino (chili flakes)
  • 2 cups conchiglie or any shape of pasta
  • 2 cups water or broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps:

  1. Using a medium pan, heat up olive oil, over medium-high heat. Once the oil is ready, sauté garlic and onion, until onions are slightly soft.
  2. Add ground pork. Mix. Let the meat cook a bit (until most of the ground pork has changed color). Add fennel sausage. Mix. Add carrots and celery. Mix and let cook for a couple of minutes.
  3. Transfer to your Clay pot. Add tomato sauce, salt, bay leaves, pepper and peperoncino. Mix to combine.
  4. Set on “soup” to cook for 1.5 to 2 hours. Or on “slow” to cook for 1-1.5 hours (Note: don’t forget to stir it occasionally to make sure it does not stick in the bottom of the pot to burn)
  5. Add conchiglie pasta and water in your clay pot.
  6. Set on stew to cook for 30 minutes.
